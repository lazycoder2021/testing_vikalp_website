What is Vikalp? What does it offer?

Vikalp WebDev school of Vikalp Solutions offers free web development lessons for coding enthusiasts. The creators of Vikalp Web Dev school firmly believe that education is a fundamental right and that it should be available for free. With this in mind, there are about to launch their YouTube channel soon, which will feature various lessons related to web development. Moreover, they also plan to hold online as well as physical classes shortly.

Web Dev Courses Vikalp Offers? - Part I

Vikalp covers everything related to web development. This means you will find frontend, backend, and even database-related lessons. You can either go for the full-stack or choose to specialize in the field you are interested in. Vikalp is not going to offer you any advice - unless you ask for it - as to what suits you the best. Because, as you probably realize by now, Vikalp firmly believes that as long as stay curious, you can learn anything.

Web Dev Courses Vikalp Offers? - Part II

Here the students are introduced to the backend. They'll learn how a NodeJS server works through the first three modules (Nodejs Basics, Intermediate, and Advanced). Then they are finally introduced to the MERN stack as a whole. This means they get to connect the React frontend with the Express server (that is built on top of NodeJs) and with MongoDB. (Of course, students can directly jump into server-side programming, if they wish to do so. This is just an ideal flow we suggest for absolute beginners.
