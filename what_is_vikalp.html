What is Vikalp? What does it offer?


Vikalp WebDev school of Vikalp Solutions offers free web development lessons for coding enthusiasts. The creators of Vikalp Web Dev school firmly believe that education is a fundamental right and that it should be available for free. With this in mind, there are about to launch their YouTube channel soon, which will feature various lessons related to web development. Moreover, they also plan to hold online as well as physical classes shortly.


Learn Web Development The Vikalp Way

Everyone can code - that is the motive of Vikalp Web Dev school. It doesn't believe in the fact that coding is only for great minds. To prove this theory, the Vikalp Web Dev school is coming up with a revolutionary curriculum that will ensure those who are willing to learn web development - irrespective of whether they possess the required aptitude or not - end up learning it. Glimpses of this curriculum can also be witnessed in their soon-to-be-launched YouTube channel.


What Does It Take To Become A Vikalpian?

Desire to learn is all it takes. That's it. Vikalp does not intend to hold entrance exams nor is it going to ask thousands of questions before someone is deemed good enough to be a student - or a vikalpian. As long as there is a burning desire in you to learn, Vikalp Web Dev school will stand by your side, holding your hands and guiding you in the right direction. All Vikalp requires of its students is to develop an undying curiosity for learning.


When Will Vikalp Web Dev School Be Launched?

The makers are trying hard to release the lessons as soon as possible. First, the launch of Vikalp Web Dev school's YouTube channel will happen. You can expect this to happen in another 3 months. Then Vikalp's web development course (both frontend and backend) will follow. At best, you can expect everything to be released in another 6 months' time. Once everything goes online, the physical classes will follow.
